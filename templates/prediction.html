Diabetes Risk Factors and Statistics

Age Risk Factors

As your age increases, the higher your risk for diabetes. After the age of 45, your risk for diabetes increase dramatically. With the aging process, your body's insulin resistance increases leading to impaired pancreatic function. People tend to exercise less as they age, which can lead to weight gain, one of the factors that have the most impact on diabetes risk.
